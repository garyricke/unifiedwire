Concept A

Classic Formula

Mirrors the proven Encore Wire layout — light gray background, wire spool on a pallet as the hero image, and a dark navy band at the bottom anchoring the tagline and contact details. Familiar structure that rep groups and distributors immediately recognize as professional and credible.

  • Easiest for the Ohio rep group to visualize in context
  • Spool-on-pallet imagery = unmistakable wire industry identity
  • Clean, no-distraction hierarchy: logo → product → CTA
  • Lowest friction for print shop production
B Click to enlarge
Concept B

Bold & Aggressive

Dark navy background with a diagonal orange-to-blue energy streak and a burst of copper wire strands radiating from the center. This is a brand-first design — built to stop traffic, command attention at trade shows, and make competitors nervous. High-contrast, high-voltage energy.

  • Maximum visibility at distance — dominates the road
  • Orange + navy + blue = full UWC brand color story
  • Wire burst imagery is distinctive, not generic
  • Strong tradeshow / event presence
C Click to enlarge
Concept C

Premium Copper

Full-bleed cinematic close-up of copper wire coils fills the entire panel. A semi-transparent navy band at top holds the logo and company name; a solid navy band at bottom anchors the tagline and contact. The product IS the design — warm, rich copper tones convey quality and craftsmanship without saying a word.

  • Most premium / aspirational feel of the three
  • The copper texture communicates quality instantly
  • Minimal text = maximum visual impact at highway speed
  • Differentiates sharply from every competitor wrap in the market
D Click to enlarge
Concept D

Glass Door

A trompe l'oeil illusion — the rear door appears to be made of clear glass, revealing the trailer packed floor-to-ceiling with rows of wire spools receding into the distance. Warm amber lighting inside the trailer makes the copper and colored wire glow. Nobody else in the industry has a wrap like this.

  • Stops traffic — people will do a double-take
  • Tells the whole story: UWC carries everything, in depth
  • Conversation starter at every jobsite and tradeshow
  • Most distinctive and sharable of all four concepts
