## SKIN COAT TECHNOLOGY

*This is a proprietary differentiator — competitive analysis recommends a white paper / dedicated feature. Key facts:*

- **Process:** Main extruder + 2 smaller co-extruders working simultaneously
  - Main extruder applies inner layer of natural compound
  - Co-extruders alternately apply outer layer of colored compound
  - Outer skin thickness: 0.004 to 0.010 mils (depending on wire size/type)
  - Two layers applied simultaneously in molten state under pressure — fused into one form
- **Result:** Color cannot be removed; will not scrape off or peel off
- **Performance:** Physical and electrical properties identical to conventionally produced PVC insulated wire
- **Standards met:** UL, CSA, and SAE
- **Business benefits:**
  - Lower minimum order quantities
  - Better pricing (reduced scrap rates, less machine downtime)
  - Custom color combinations made practical
