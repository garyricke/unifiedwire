Design Notes
Print Specs

Each banner is built at the exact 33″ × 81″ aspect ratio with 1″ top and 3″ bottom safety margins honored. Use the toolbar toggle above to overlay the margin guides.

Typography

All banner text is live HTML — nothing is rasterized. Typography scales with container width so the layout holds up at print resolution. Headings use Barlow Condensed; tagline and body use DM Sans.

Next Step

Once a direction is approved, we'll export each banner as a print-ready PDF at 150 DPI (4950 × 12150 px) with backgrounds at full resolution and safety-margin guides hidden.
