Who We Are

The Full Manufacturing Picture

From copper rod to finished wire, UWC owns the entire production process — delivering consistent quality and fast turnaround without the middleman.

Made in USA

All manufacturing takes place at our DeKalb, Illinois facility. We're proud to support American jobs and domestic supply chains.

Vertically Integrated

We draw our own copper rod, extrude our own insulation, and finish every product in-house — for tighter quality control and shorter lead times.

ISO 9001:2015 Certified

Our quality management system is independently certified, ensuring every spool shipped meets rigorous, consistent manufacturing standards.

Product Lines

Wire & Cable for Every Application

Eight specialized product categories covering construction, automotive, marine, industrial, and custom OEM requirements.

01 — Building & Lighting

Building & Lighting Wire

High-performance THHN, TFN, and TFFN wire for residential and commercial electrical installations, available through 750 MCM.

THHN TFN / TFFN to 750 MCM
02 — Automotive

Automotive Wire

SAE J1128-compliant XLPE and primary wire for OEM and aftermarket vehicle wiring harnesses. GXL, SXL, and TXL constructions available.

GXL / SXL / TXL GPT Primary SAE J1128
03 — Marine & Boat

Marine & Boat Cable

Tinned copper and GPTM wire engineered for corrosion resistance in wet, saltwater, and harsh marine environments.

GPTM Tinned Copper Corrosion Resistant
04 — Hook-Up Wire

UL Style & Hook-Up Wire

A broad catalog of UL-listed hook-up wire styles for electronics, appliances, and control panel applications.

UL 1028 UL 1007 / 1569 UL 1061
05 — Power & Battery

Power Distribution & Battery Cable

Heavy-duty SGT, SGX, and STT battery cables for demanding power distribution applications in vehicles and equipment.

SGT / SGX STT Battery Heavy Gauge
06 — Specialty Cords

Specialty & Flexible Cords

Portable and flexible power cords including SOOW and STOW constructions, plus ripcord and parallel-bonded appliance wire.

SOOW / STOW Portable Cord Ripcord
07 — Custom OEM

Custom OEM & Industrial

Factory-direct custom constructions for specialized applications including elevator and hoistway cables and Mil-Spec Type B and C wire.

Mil-Spec Hoistway / Elevator Custom Build

Corporate Capabilities
