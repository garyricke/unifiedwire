Our Culture

A team that teaches from the roots

At Unified Wire & Cable, experience compounds. Our production manager has been here for 33 years — he trained most of the people on the floor. That's not a statistic; that's a culture.

We believe in understanding the "why" behind every process. New team members are mentored — not just handed a procedure. Whether you're running an extrusion line or managing quality systems, you'll understand what you're doing and why it matters.
