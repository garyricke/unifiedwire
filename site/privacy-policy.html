Short version: We collect only what we need to respond to you and improve our site. We do not sell your personal information. You can ask us to delete your data at any time.

1. Who We Are

Unified Wire & Cable, Inc. ("UWC," "we," "our," or "us") is a wire and cable manufacturer headquartered at 1601 Industrial Drive, DeKalb, IL 60115. This Privacy Policy applies to the website at unifiedwire.com and any subdomains we operate (the "Site").

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in two ways:

Information you provide directly:

  • Contact & inquiry forms — name, company, email address, phone number, and message content when you submit a contact or quote request form.
  • Find-a-Rep submissions — zip code or state when you use our rep locator tool.
  • Career applications — name, email, resume, and any information you include in your application.
  • Email correspondence — messages you send to our published email addresses.

Information collected automatically:

  • Usage data — pages visited, time on site, referring URL, and browser/device type, collected via server logs and analytics.
  • IP address — used for fraud prevention and aggregated geographic analytics. We do not link IP addresses to individual identities.
  • Cookies — see the Cookies & Tracking section below.

We do not collect payment card numbers, government ID numbers, or other sensitive financial data through this Site.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries, quote requests, and customer service questions.
  • Connect you with a UWC sales representative in your area.
  • Process and evaluate employment applications.
  • Send transactional communications related to your request (e.g., confirmation that your form was received).
  • Improve the Site's content, navigation, and performance using aggregated analytics.
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our legal rights.

We do not use your information to send unsolicited marketing emails or to build advertising profiles. If we ever send a marketing communication, it will include an easy opt-out mechanism.

4. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share data only in limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — vendors who help us operate the Site (e.g., web hosting, form processing, email delivery). They may access data only to perform services on our behalf and are contractually required to protect it.
  • Sales representatives — if you use our Find-a-Rep feature, your contact information may be shared with the independent UWC sales representative covering your region so they can follow up with you.
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, subpoena, or a valid government request, or to protect the safety, rights, or property of UWC, our users, or the public.
  • Business transfers — in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of substantially all assets, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change in ownership.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use a small number of cookies necessary for the Site to function. We do not use third-party advertising or behavioral tracking cookies.

  • Session cookies — temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser, used to maintain your session state.
  • Analytics cookies — if we use a web analytics service (e.g., a self-hosted or privacy-focused tool), it may set a first-party cookie to distinguish unique visits. No cross-site tracking is performed.
  • Preference cookies — may store lightweight UI preferences (e.g., sidebar state) locally in your browser.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Doing so may affect some Site functionality but will not prevent you from accessing content.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected:

  • Contact & quote inquiries — up to 3 years, or until you request deletion.
  • Career applications — up to 1 year from the date of submission, unless a position is filled sooner.
  • Server logs — up to 90 days for security and performance monitoring.
  • Analytics data — aggregated and anonymized; retained indefinitely in aggregate form.

When retention periods expire, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

7. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion — request that we delete your personal data, subject to legal retention obligations.
  • Opt-out — opt out of any marketing communications at any time.
  • Data portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format where technically feasible.

California residents: Under the CCPA, you have the right to know, delete, and opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address in the Contact Us section. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Security

We use industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information — including HTTPS encryption for all data in transit and access controls limiting who can view submitted data. No internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. If you have reason to believe your interaction with us is no longer secure, please notify us immediately.

9. Third-Party Links

The Site may contain links to third-party websites (e.g., industry associations, standards bodies, distributor sites). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies before providing any personal information.

10. Children

This Site is intended for business and professional use and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal information to us, contact us and we will promptly delete it.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For material changes, we will provide additional notice (such as a banner on the Site homepage). Continued use of the Site after any change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
