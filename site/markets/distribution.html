Why Distributors Choose UWC

The manufacturer that works like your partner

Most distributors carry products from multiple manufacturers. The ones that earn preferred-supplier status make life easier — spec confirmation fast, stock on hand, and minimums that don't force you to overcommit inventory.

Emergency stock fulfillment

We maintain sizable finished goods inventory. When your customer has a line-down situation, our team can ship from stock — often same day or next day.

Lower minimums

Our Skin Coat co-extrusion technology reduces scrap and changeover costs — enabling lower minimum order quantities on color-coded wire without a pricing penalty.

100+ UL AWM approved styles

The breadth of our UL listing portfolio means fewer sourcing gaps in your catalog — from standard hook-up wire to specialty constructions.

Real people, fast response

You reach an experienced team — not a call center. Our sales and customer service team has the tenure and knowledge to answer technical questions and turn quotes quickly.

Vertical integration = supply security

We fabricate copper in-house. When raw material markets tighten, we don't rely on outside suppliers — which means your orders are more protected from disruption.

50 years of stability

Founded in 1977. The same family leadership. A production manager who has been here since near the beginning. Distributors can count on us being here — and on continuity of product and quality.
