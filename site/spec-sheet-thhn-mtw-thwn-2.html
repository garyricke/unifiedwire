Standards

UL-83, UL-1063, UL-1581, UL-1685, UL-2556; AWM Spec 1316; ASTM Stranding Class B3, B8; CSA C22.2 No. 75; NEMA WC70 / ICEA S-95-658; UL VW-1 Flame Test; UL 1685-FT4 / Flame Test (1/0 AWG and larger); REACH and RoHS Compliant.

Conductor

Stranded, bare copper conductor manufactured in accordance with ASTM B3, B8.

Insulation

Flexible Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC), heat- and moisture-resistant, flame-retardant compound per UL-83 and UL-1063.

Applications

Type THHN/THWN-2 building wire is approved by the NEC for general purpose use in new construction or rewiring up to 600 volts. THHN/THWN-2 conductors are suitable for wet or dry locations at temperatures up to 90° C, or up to 75° C when exposed to oil or coolants. When used as Type MTW, the conductor is rated for dry locations at 90° C and for wet or oil-exposed locations at temperatures up to 60° C.
