Detailed Spec Sheet

GPTM — Marine Primary Wire

Made in USA UL Listed RoHS Compliant ISO 9001:2015 REACH

Standards

SAE J378 / SAE J1128 GPTM.

Conductor

Stranded, tinned copper conductor — tinning provides corrosion resistance in wet, saltwater, and harsh marine environments.

Insulation

Polyvinyl chloride (PVC), flame-retardant compound, 80° C rating.

Applications

For use as low-voltage primary wire in marine vessels and recreational boats at a nominal system voltage of 60 V DC (25 V AC) or less. Engineered for the harsh wet and saltwater environments encountered in boat hulls, bilges, engine compartments, and exposed deck wiring runs. Compatible with ABYC marine wiring practices.
