Standards

UL 44 — Type XHHW-2 / SIS; UL 1581; UL 2556; ASTM B3, B8 — Bare Copper Conductors; ICEA S-95-658 / NEMA WC-70; CSA C22.2 No. 38 — RW90; NFPA 70 (NEC) Article 310; UL 1685 Flame Test (1/0 AWG or larger); UL VW-1 Flame Test (14 AWG to 4/0 AWG); RoHS & REACH Compliant.

Conductor

Stranded, bare copper conductor manufactured in accordance with ASTM B3, B8.

Insulation

Smooth XLPE / XLPO insulation for easier pulling per UL-44.

Applications

Type XHHW-2 copper building wire is designed for general-purpose power distribution and may be installed in conduit, tubing, underground raceways, and other NEC-recognized wiring methods for services, feeders, and branch circuits. It is also appropriate for low-leakage applications, with a dielectric constant of 3.5 or below. 8 AWG and larger are also rated sunlight-resistant. Cables are compliant with UL’s FT-2 Flame Test. Sizes 1/0 AWG and larger meet the UL-1685 Method 1 flame test for CT-rated installations. Type SIS conductors are acceptable for switchgear and switchboard wiring and use flame-retardant thermoset insulation rated for operation up to 90° C.
