Detailed Spec Sheet

TFN / TFFN — Fixture Wire

Made in USA UL Listed RoHS Compliant ISO 9001:2015 REACH

Standards

UL 66, UL 1316, UL 1452, CSA TEW, NFPA 70 (NEC).

Conductor

Solid bare copper conductor (TFN) or stranded bare copper conductor (TFFN), 16 and 18 AWG.

Insulation

Polyvinyl chloride (PVC) primary insulation with an extruded nylon outer jacket. Multi-rated thermoplastic compound.

Applications

Engineered for fixture wiring inside lighting fixtures, control circuits in cabinets, and small-gauge interior wiring runs in conduit. Suitable for use in dry locations at temperatures up to 90°C and rated 600 V. Common in commercial and industrial luminaire builds, panel-board feeds to ballasts, and transformer secondaries.
