2026-07-21
Tuesday, July 21
USA / America-250 edition logo added to the Brand Guide Brand
At the customer's request, added a patriotic stars-and-stripes treatment of the UWC mark to the Brand Guide logo section (§04), in its own "USA Edition — America's 250th" subsection kept deliberately separate from the three core lockups so the primary identity system stays intact. Two downloadable variants: the USA mark alone, and the mark with "Unified Wire & Cable" locked up beneath it — composed by reusing the exact brand wordmark vector at the primary lockup's proportions, so the typography is identical. The wordmark navy was matched to the mark's own outline (#0C2F66) so the two read as one color. Each variant is delivered as SVG, transparent PNG, and vector PDF, hosted on Cloudinary (uwc/logo/uwc-usa-2026 and uwc/logo/uwc-usa-2026-name) and served the same way as the existing lockups. Pushed straight to main; live.

Built a gated internal coordination hub (spec-navigator-data-plan.html, password copper) to unblock the Spec Navigator by finally collecting real product/SKU data from Unified. Registered in the internal-pages menu (uwc.js); noindex and kept out of public nav/search. Sent an intro email to Jason and Ebaa (cc Tom, Les, Jamie) routing them to the page and the tracker.

Nav fixes — Markets link + Resources dropdown Launch
Two items from Jason's June 9 review of the live site, fixed and pushed straight to main.
