Detailed Spec Sheet

AL 25 kV MV Power Cable — SS-260 EPR, PVC Jacket

Made in USA UL Listed RoHS Compliant ISO 9001:2015 REACH

Standards

ASTM B801, ASTM B231, ASTM B836, UL 1072, UL 1685, CSA C22.2 No. 230, CSA C22.2 No. 2556, AEIC CS-8, NFPA 70 (NEC).

Conductor and Conductor Shield

Compressed concentric aluminum conductor in accordance with ASTM B801 and B231, incorporating an extruded thermoset semiconducting conductor shield.

Insulation and Insulation Shield

Lead-free Ethylene Propylene Rubber (EPR) insulation, 260-mil nominal wall (133% level), with strippable semiconducting shield.

Tape Shield

Copper tape applied helically with a nominal thickness of 5 mils and a minimum 25% overlap.

Jacketing

Polyvinyl chloride (PVC) jacket.

Applications

Suitable for use in wet and dry environments and for direct burial installations when installed with a grounding conductor in close proximity, in accordance with NEC. The construction provides resistance to sunlight and moisture, exhibits high resistance to corona discharge, and offers protection against a broad range of chemicals, oils, and acids. Flame-retardant properties are incorporated. The cable is suitable for aerial installations when supported by a messenger where superior electrical performance is required. Cable Tray (CT) rating applies for sizes 1/0 AWG and larger.
