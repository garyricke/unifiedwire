Proprietary Technology

Skin Coat Technology

A state-of-the-art co-extrusion process that applies a precision outer color skin — 0.004" to 0.010" thick — fused to the inner compound in a single pass.

  • Color is fused in the melt — it won't peel, scrape off, or separate
  • Meets all UL, CSA, and SAE standards — same electrical and physical properties as conventional PVC
  • Enables lower minimums and better pricing through reduced scrap and machine downtime
