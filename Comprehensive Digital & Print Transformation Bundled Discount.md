# **Unified Wire & Cable: Comprehensive Digital & Print Transformation**

## **Executive Summary**

Transitioning Unified Wire & Cable's digital presence and physical sales collateral simultaneously ensures a highly cohesive brand rollout ahead of the upcoming May tradeshow. By running the website build and the spec sheet modernization concurrently, we eliminate redundant labor, consolidate strategic planning, and leverage overlapping AI asset generation to deliver enterprise-grade marketing capabilities at a highly efficient bundled rate.

---

## **1\. Project Synergies & Efficiency Gains**

Fulfilling these two scopes of work under a single timeline unlocks significant cost savings:

* **Consolidated Media Capture:** The on-site plant and people photography utilizing an iPhone only needs to be executed once. This captures the foundation for the website's visual build while entirely eliminating the redundant $1,000 to $1,300 on-site photoshoot labor originally quoted for the spec sheets.

* **Unified AI Asset Production:** The structural grounding captured during the single iPhone shoot will be fed into the Nano Banana AI model to generate hyper-realistic, 4K industrial environments for both the web pages and the printed collateral.  
* **Streamlined Data Migration:** We will centralize your technical data into an editable Google Sheet. This single source of truth will power both the dynamic, auto-updating web spec sheets and the clean technical data tables on the reverse side of the printed collateral.


## **2\. Combined Scope of Work**

### **Phase A: Digital Transformation (Website)**

We will deliver a modern, comprehensive new website on Squarespace.

* **Strategic Architecture:** A streamlined, 7-key landing page structure including Home, Products (Mega-Menu), Markets & Industries, Resources & Engineering Tools, Services & Capabilities, About Us, and Contact Us.  
* **Dynamic Spec Sheets:** A web page spec sheet system driven by an editable Google Sheet that updates automatically within 5 minutes, allowing visitors to download instantly updated PDFs.  
* **Brand Modernization:** A logo refresh focusing on simplification, choosing from three proposed digital-friendly options.  
* **Launch Campaign:** A proactive email campaign to announce the new site.

### **Phase B: Premium Spec Sheet Suite**

We will transition UWC from text-heavy technical sheets to a high-end, visual-first collateral suite.

* **Design & Architecture:** Eight (8) distinct, double-sided $8.5" \\times 11"$ designs featuring high-fidelity, "lifestyle" industrial photography on the front and technical data tables on the reverse.

* **Categories:** Including Corporate Capabilities, Automotive Solutions, Marine & Boat Cable, Building & Lighting, UL Style & Hook-Up, Power & Battery, Specialty Cords, and Custom OEM & Industrial.

## **3\. Accelerated Merged Timeline**

To ensure both the digital platform and physical collateral are fully launched and delivered in time for the May tradeshow, we will adhere to the following concurrent timeline:

* **Late February:** Finalize site structure, approve the logo modernization, and execute the single on-site plant and photography capture.  
* **March:** Build out the Squarespace platform, engineer the dynamic Google Sheet-to-PDF infrastructure, and execute AI drafting and refinement for the print suite.  
* **Early/Mid-April:** Finalize staff video edits, integrate the 360-degree plant capture, and secure final design approvals for the print spec sheets by April 10\.  
* **Late April:** Apply massive human oversight and QA to all AI-assisted generation and stage the email announcement campaign.  
* **First Week of May:** Website officially launches and physical print delivery is guaranteed for the trade show.
